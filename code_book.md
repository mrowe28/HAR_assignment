##Code book for variables included in a tidy version of the Human Activity Recognition Using Smartphones Dataset##


###Included below are descriptions of each variable output by the script contained in 'run_analysis.R' when it is applied to the UCI HAR dataset.  Numbering indicates column position within the resulting data frame.###

__1) Subject__ - research subject identification number

__2) Activity__ - activity performed while measurements were taken

__3) tBodyAcc.mean.X__ - an average of the mean body linear accelerometer time domain signals in the X direction, for the specified subject and activity

__4) tBodyAcc.mean.Y__ - an average of the mean body linear accelerometer time domain signals in the Y direction, for the specified subject and activity

__5) tBodyAcc.mean.Z__ - an average of the mean body linear accelerometer time domain signals in the Z direction, for the specified subject and activity

__6) tBodyAcc.std.X__ - an average of the standard deviation among body linear accelerometer time domain signals in the X direction, for the specified subject and activity

__7) tBodyAcc.std.Y__ - an average of the standard deviation among body linear accelerometer time domain signals in the Y direction, for the specified subject and activity

__8) tBodyAcc.std.Z__ - an average of the standard deviation among body linear accelerometer time domain signals in the Z direction, for the specified subject and activity
